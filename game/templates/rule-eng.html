Game Rule

In the beginning, you will be assigned to a opponent. In each round, you and your opponent choose between Cooperation(C) and Defection(D) simultaneously. After you have independently chosen, your scores will be calculated and displayed according to the matrix on the right.

So there are four possible outcomes:
1. Your choice: C; Your opponent's choice: C;
Your payoff: 200; Your opponent's payoff: 200;

2. Your choice: C; Your opponent's choice: D;
Your payoff: -100; Your opponent's payoff: 300;

3. Your choice: D; Your opponent's choice: C;
Your payoff: 300; Your opponent's payoff: -100;

4. Your choice: D; Your opponent's choice: D;
Your payoff : 0 ; Your opponent's payoff: 0 ;

The game terminates after finite rounds. Your scores will be compared with others and showed on a ranking list. Those accomplish with top grade will be rewarded.
